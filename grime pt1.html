Continental Lift

Grime is the South African style brand pioneering in the youth fashion/entertainment space. Slow fashion change is at the brand DNA. In comparison to fast-fashion brands, this brand launches only a few collections throughout the year. Besides that, the brand encourages sustaining the time of simple clothes by redesigning them into original pieces. The /id/ brand was birthed from facing the struggle between the unstainable pressure to wear the latest and most expensive clothing &The inability to express yourself through simple yet ethical clothes. The sustainable style of the GRIME brand enables people to express themselves through simple but stylish designs and accessories. The brand has been able to create a strong. image for itself with its unique design.
